Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Seattle King Roofing LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website and communications, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including your name, phone number, email address, service address, and project details when you submit a form, email us, or call us.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to estimate requests and project inquiries
  • To schedule inspections, consultations, and service visits
  • To improve our website, customer communication, and service process
  • To maintain records related to completed or proposed work

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers or subcontractors only when necessary to help us operate our business, schedule services, or complete approved work.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use standard cookies, analytics tools, or server logs to understand site traffic and improve performance. These tools may collect non-identifying technical data such as browser type, pages visited, and general location information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we receive. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates or corrections to the personal information you previously submitted, or to ask questions about how your information is being used.

Contact Us

If you have privacy questions, contact Seattle King Roofing LLC at seattlekingroofing@gmail.com or call (425) 948-5718.
